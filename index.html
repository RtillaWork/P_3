About us

Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. is proud to bring to you this year’s Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival.

Enjoy diversified classics with family and friends from August 5th to 8th at Brooklyn Bridge Park!

View details »

Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. is a registered not-for-profit organization and the organizer of this year’s Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. Led by Jennifer Viala and a team of dedicated film enthusiats and local volunteers, we hope to use this beautiful and historical venue to bring the people of Brooklyn and Ney York City together, dicover and enjoy classics and reclaim Brooklyn Bridge Park as a family and community friendly space. This year's Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival runs from August 5th to August 8th at Brooklyn Bridge Park, a major entertainment venue in New York City that can hold up to 5,000 people and that is of both cultural and historical significance. See you there!

About Brooklyn Bridge Park

Brooklyn Bridge Park is a world-class waterfront park with rolling hills, riverfront promenades, lush gardens, and spectacular city views. Our list of amenities continues to grow as we work each day to revitalize this previously deteriorated industrial space and build a park that allows New Yorkers to rediscover the waterfront. Did you know that it is also a major entertainment venue in New York that can hold up to 5,000 people?
